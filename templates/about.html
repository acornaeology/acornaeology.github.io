About

acornaeology is a collection of resources for exploring the software and hardware of Acorn Computers — the BBC Micro, Archimedes, and related machines from the 1970s, 1980s, and 1990s.

The site brings together annotated disassemblies, technical references, and other materials to help preserve and understand the engineering behind these systems.
